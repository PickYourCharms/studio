Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

At PickYourCharms, we respect your privacy. PickYourCharms is a brand owned and operated by Adamantine Ventures (GSTIN: 19MVJPS1333J1ZX). This policy explains how we handle your information when you build your jewelry and place an order.

1. What We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to fulfill your order:

  • Contact Info: Name, Phone Number, Email Address.
  • Shipping Info: Delivery Address, Landmark, City, State, Pincode.
  • Order Details: The specific jewelry designs (charms, bases) you select.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Order Processing: To pack and ship your custom jewelry.
  • Communication: We use WhatsApp to confirm orders and send payment details.
  • Shipping: We share your address and phone number with our courier partners (e.g., Delhivery, Shiprocket) strictly for delivery purposes.

3. Where Your Data Lives

Google Sheets: Your order details are securely stored in our internal order management system hosted on Google Drive.

Local Storage: Your cart items and draft designs are saved locally on your own device (in your browser) until you place the order.

4. Payments

We do not store or process credit card or banking information directly on this website. Payments are handled via UPI or Payment Links shared securely over WhatsApp.

5. Your Rights

You can request to have your customer data deleted from our records at any time after your order is delivered.
